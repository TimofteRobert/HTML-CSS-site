Motherboard

The motherboard is what connects all the other parts of your pc, allowing them to interract. There are different types which are compatible with just some of the cases, so be careful!

Motherboards can have multiple slots for RAM, GPUs, can support multiple HDD and SDD, integrated GPUs.

The bigger size of the motherboards means that they have more space, but that comes at the cost of needing a bigger PC case.
