Keyboard

A computer keyboard is a peripheral input device modeled after the typewriter keyboard. They typically have a set of characters engraved or printed on them and each press of a key corresponds to a single written symbol.

They come in different types, desktop size, laptop size, flexible, handheld, thumb sized, multifunctional, wired, wireless even a device projecting the keyboard.

Afordable and necessary unless you are using a touchscreen keyboard.
