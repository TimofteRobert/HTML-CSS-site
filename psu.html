Powersource(PSU)

They come in different sizes with different power standards, so you need to know the size of the space the case can store the powersource in.

Powersources can be Non-modular, Semi-modular or Modular. Thatmeans that they come with the cables nonremovable or removable.

There are many cables but that doesn't mean you have to use all of them. In the next image they are shown by name and what it powers.

They come rated and the next image shows the efficiency.

Not the most costly part of the PC but an important one, when you build your computer you need to know how much power the components use so you can buy a powersource that can give them that power.
