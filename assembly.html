Assembly steps

Step 1 in putting your computer together is preparing the Case.

Step 2 put CPU and Cooler on the Motherboard.

Step 3 installing RAM on the Motherboard.

Step 4 mounting the Motherboard to the Case.

Step 5 Storage installation which is HDD or SSD or even both.

Step 6 GPU installation.

Step 7 PSU installation.

Step 8 connect the components.

Final step is Boot Up.

There are video tutorials that show how to these steps if just reading is not enough.
