Graphics Processing Unit(GPU)

There are motherboards with integrated GPUs, but they are not powerful enough for many of the things some people use a PC for.

A good website to use if you want to buy a graphics card for a gaming pc is this one:

VideoCardBenchmark

A website to use if you want to buy a graphics card for a workstation pc is this one:

Benchmark.Chaos

They come in different dimensions so the dimensions of the case and motherboard need to be spacious enough.

The most knows are NVIDIA and AMD, they control almost 100% of the market.

For NVIDIA there are 3 types of graphics cards:

NVIDIA GeForce is a brand for gaming and for creators, it has under it NVIDIA GeForce RTX, NVIDIA GeForce GTX, NVIdia GeForce MX and NVIDIA GeForce GT. NVIDIA RTX is the brand for for professional and visual computing which has superseded NVIDIA Quadro.

They have CUDA which is better for video editing or 3D rendering which is better for a workstation pc as of this year. If AMD release graphics cards good enough for a workstation PC then they could be another choice, but as of now it's better to have an NVIDIA card.

AMD has just one brand of graphics cards which is RX.

Most GPUs are designed for a specific usage, real-time 3D graphics or other mass calculations.

These are the most expensive parts of a PC to buy usually, that does not mean that you have to pay for the most expensive one if you do not need the power achieved with them.
