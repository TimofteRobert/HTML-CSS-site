Case

There are different types of cases which you can choose, each with their own benefits and downsides. In the next image there are the dimensions of different types of cases.

Of course these are basic in aspect, there are others with tubes for cooling fluid, rgb lights and so on, you can change them yourself.

This is an important component where you have to be careful what you choose, the dimensions tell you how much space you have, the insides tell you where to put the other parts from your pc and how many of them you can put, from fans to graphics cards to ssd to hdd and so on.

There is not that much else you have to know about cases if you are not passionate about the aspect of your pc, because the case if one of the parts most looked at after some of the other periferals.
