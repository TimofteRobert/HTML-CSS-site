Hard Disk Drive(HDD)

HDD is a data storage device that stores and retrieves digital data using magnetic storage and one or more rigid rapidly rotating platters coated with magnetic material.

They are slowly being replaced by SSDs because they have better speed, power consumption, smaller zise, higher capacity and durability.

SSds have four to nine times higher cost per bit, but the cost is falling.
