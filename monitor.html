Monitor

The part of the PC that most of the time attracts attention. They changed as technology advanced and they will continue to change.

Not only the external aspect of them changed, the number of pixels they can display changed.

The aspect ratios of monitors are different too along with them being the usual or curved.

For yourself you should be careful that the parts you buy can support the type of monitor you want to buy. Powerful components for a weak monitor with low resolution is bad but weak components for a powerful monitor is worse.

Another thing you should think about is the space you have available on your desk, if you have enough and the graphics cards can support it you can use multiple monitors too.

Depending on which you bought first you should check the types of cables, some of them are not compatible.
